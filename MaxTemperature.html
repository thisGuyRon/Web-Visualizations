Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 degrees latitude). More interestingly however is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This might be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year that this data was gathered.
